Mission

Alliance Francophone of the Palm Beaches mission is to offer cross-cultural opportunities to build harmonious and diverse relationships and to promote understanding and mutual respect for the cultural diversity within the Palm Beach community.

There are more than 300 million people in the world who share the language and culture of France, from countries such as Canada, Switzerland, Algeria, Lebanon, Belgium, and Haiti. The population of Palm Beach County comprises a large group of these multicultural families whose first language is not English and who and are in need of a collective outlet to share their experiences.

Furthermore, as fellow Francophone’s, we hope to develop projects in aid of impoverished communities in French speaking countries. There are over 70 countries around the world that speak French and most of them are struggling. Finally, our mission is to preserve the cultures, languages and communities in Francophone countries.

Alliance Francophone provides a comprehensive array of culturally relevant opportunities to bridge the gap. We offer free French literacy classes, lectures by notable authors, movies, museum events, informative communal dinners and linkage to Francophone branches throughout the world to children and adults in the Palm Beach County community.

Social Events

The Alliance organizes social events and provides a forum for members and prospective members to converse in French, enjoy French food, and celebrate the French culture with the Francophone community at large.
